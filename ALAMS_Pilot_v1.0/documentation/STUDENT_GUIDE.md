# ALAMS Student Quick Start Guide

This document describes how students unlock workstations and record class attendance using the **ALAMS (Aurxon Lab Access Management System)**.

---

## 1. Unlocking Your Workstation

Each restricted student computer displays the ALAMS Lock Screen. Follow these steps to log in:

### Step 1: Scan the QR Code
1.  Open your mobile phone camera or a barcode scanner app.
2.  Scan the dynamic QR code displayed on the center of the workstation screen.
3.  The scanned link will redirect you to the **Student Portal** page in your browser.

### Step 2: Log in on Your Phone
1.  Log in to the portal using your enrollment credentials:
    *   **Enrollment ID**: `ENR2026001` (or your assigned enrollment ID)
    *   **Password**: `Student@2026!`
2.  Upon successful login, a 6-digit **One-Time Session PIN** will display on your phone screen.
3.  *Note: The session PIN is only valid for 60 seconds.*

### Step 3: Enter PIN on Workstation
1.  Type the 6-digit PIN code on the workstation screen.
2.  Press **Verify PIN**.
3.  The lock screen will hide, the Windows Desktop (explorer.exe) will load, and you can begin your lab session.

---

## 2. Running Your Session & The Session Widget

Once unlocked, a small floating **Session Widget** will display in the top-right corner of your desktop:
*   It displays your active enrollment ID and session duration.
*   **WARNING**: Do not close or alter the widget files.
*   If you step away or finish early, click **Log Out** on the widget.

---

## 3. Logging Out

At the end of your class:
1.  Click **Log Out** on the floating Session Widget.
2.  The Windows desktop will close, saving active files, and the workstation will return to the secure ALAMS Lock Screen.
3.  Your check-out time is saved, and your attendance is logged.
